## Accessibility

<section id="accessibility" className="sb-unstyled scroll-mt-2xl">
  <p className="text-body-1 text-on-neutral-container mb-md">
    Accessibility is **the ability for applications to be used by everyone**, including people with
    vision, auditory, motor or cognitive impairments.
  </p>
  <p className="text-body-2 text-on-neutral-container mb-lg">
    Spark UI addresses key aspects: screen reader support, keyboard navigation, semantics and
    [WAI-ARIA](https://www.w3.org/TR/wai-aria-1.2) patterns. Our components align with **[WCAG 2.1
    Level AA](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21)** and the [ARIA Authoring Practices
    Guide](https://www.w3.org/WAI/ARIA/apg/).
  </p>
  <p className="text-body-2 text-on-neutral-container mb-lg">
    We test with Testing Library, Playwright, Axe, VoiceOver and TalkBack—locally and in CI. Beyond
    helping users with disabilities, good a11y often improves the experience for everyone (e.g.
    keyboard-only use).
  </p>
  <Callout kind="warning" marginY="medium">
    Accessibility can be broken during implementation. Preserve focus management, labels and
    semantics when integrating Spark into your app.
  </Callout>
  <p className="text-body-2 text-on-neutral-container mt-md">
    Useful resources: [Testing Library](https://testing-library.com/),
    [Playwright](https://playwright.dev/), [Axe](https://www.deque.com/axe/).
  </p>
</section>
